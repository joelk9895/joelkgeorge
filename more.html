This is the glimpse of my life

This is scrollable

I was born in March 4th 2003 in Kundara Kerala.

My family migrated to Delhi after I was born.

I was at Delhi for about a year.

We then came back to Kerala when I was one and half years old.

I am not boasting but my mother used to say that I was a fast learner.

I joined SNGCS at the age of 4.

I had a interest for science and math from my childhood.

I was eager to know about space

When I was at 8th standard I had my first computer.

When I was at 9th standard I got interested in programming
and started developing windows app using visual basic.

During the vacation I went to Chennai. The trip enriched my skills
and learnt new things that were on the bucket list.

I was selected to the church choir.

I went for keyboard classes.But I only went for few classes and
learnt upto C minor. I told you that I was a fast learner
I learnt rest of the piano by myself(without using youtube or other tutorials).
How I did this was by practicing. I realized that the rest of the scale
was same but different home key. I am right now the keyboard player at my church and for a band.

I became good at drawing after the Chennai trip as
I went for drawing class and trained under a reputed artist in Chennai.

I learnt journalism when I was at Chennai.
I learnt how to frame news article.
The class was taken by a reputed English newspaper company.

During the stay at Chennai I learnt swimming.
I enrolled for a 14days course. But I started swimming at the 5th day.
My coach said that I have the skill to go for national competitions.
But I couldn't go to competitions as there were no pools in my locality for me to practice.

I had passed my 10th standard with 95% mark
and had 4.0 converted to GPA which is out of 4.
10.0 in CGPA

During my 10th vacation,I went for a bridgecourse
as It will be boon for my academics. Because in Indian education system
there is a huge transition in the complexity of academics from 10th to 11th class.
11th class contains a tough curriculum. Bridgecourse eased my transition.

I was made to attend medical batch, But I know I liked engineering,
I was bad at biology as I was bad at remembering things.
So I told my parents and joined computer science. Even though I joined late to computer science.
I was the best programmer. My computer science teacher used to say
I should go to a reputed engineering college and pursue computer enginnering
as I have a instinct for programming inborn.

During the 11th vacation I applied for research at IIT palakkad.
But they cancelled it due to the pandemic

I decided to improve my programming skills
so I attended courses from coursera and MIT opencourseware. and
earned a certificate from University of Michigan and google.
I got specialization in Python and machine learning using tensorflow.

My class for 12th started but was through zoom.
During that time I got an idea to digitilize education and
made a program using Python that collect answer from student and
compare the answer with the answer the database using Natural Language Processing
and give a score based on the answer.
This system was aimed to conduct online subjective exams
